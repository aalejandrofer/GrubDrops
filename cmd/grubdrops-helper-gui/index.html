Authorize a Kick account

Reads your browser's kick.com cookies and uploads them to the miner. Make sure you're signed in to Kick in your usual browser first.
(Twitch needs no helper — it authorizes via device code on the miner.)

step 1 · sign in
A new tab opens. Sign in there if you're not already, then return here.

cookies → miner. No data leaves your machine otherwise.
